Questions

Before You Visit

How old do you have to be? +
You must be 18 years or older with a valid ID to enter Star Light Cafe. This is Virginia state law for tobacco establishments.
Can I bring my own food? +
Yes. Outside food is welcome. Bring dinner, snacks, whatever you like. Outside drinks are not allowed, but we have fresh juices, smoothies, and Moroccan mint tea.
What hookah brands do you carry? +
We carry Al Fakher (11 flavors) and Starbuzz (9 flavors). Al Fakher is our classic line. Starbuzz is the premium option with bolder, more unique flavor profiles.
How much does hookah cost? +
Al Fakher starts at $10+tax per person. Starbuzz starts at $15+tax per person. For two people sharing, both brands are $27+tax. Disposable hoses are $3 each.
Do you have Wi-Fi? +
Yes. Free Wi-Fi for all customers. Many of our regulars bring laptops and work from here.
Is there a non-smoking area? +
Star Light is primarily a hookah lounge, so smoking is present throughout. Non-smoking customers are welcome but there is a $5 charge on Fridays and Saturdays.
What are your hours? +
We open at 4 PM every day. Monday through Thursday and Sunday we close at midnight. Friday and Saturday we stay open until 2 AM.
Do I need a reservation? +
No reservation needed. Walk-ins are always welcome. For large groups on Friday or Saturday nights, calling ahead is a good idea so we can have your setup ready.
